Remember

Stash distractions away for later.





Supported platforms: macOS Catalina and higher

Add Reminders

Activate Remember by hitting ⌥⎵, type whatever it is you need to keep track of, hit return and then go about your business.

Manage Your Entries

Use the arrow keys to navigate through your pending entries and archive ones you've completed with ⌫.

Get Notified

Add due dates to your entries using intuitive modifiers (like +1d or @10am mon) to get notified when they're due.
